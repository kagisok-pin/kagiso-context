## ADHD & Mental Health

**ADHD Diagnosis:** Formal diagnosis received (September 2025). Kagiso's response was immediate and systems-focused — redesigned his operating environment rather than relying on willpower.

**Non-Negotiable ADHD Operating Principles:**
1. One task list. One calendar. Documents in one place. Everything else feeds those.
2. Capture is frictionless (voice/text in 2 taps); triage on schedule, not ad hoc.
3. Time budgeted by default (auto time-boxing), not "when I get to it."
4. Guardrails for attention: Focus modes, site blockers, visible task queues.
5. Automate anything that happens twice.

**Cognitive Patterns to Note:**
- Likely struggles with perfectionism (all-or-nothing thinking) and catastrophizing under stress.
- Actively working with a therapist on cognitive distortions; uses the CBT correction protocol: trigger → name distortion → evidence test → balanced thought → reality-test action.
- Has worked with Imago Dialogue technique in couples context (mirror → validate → empathize). Views conflicts as process breakdowns, not someone being "wrong."

**Memory & Focus Recovery:**
- After a period of high stress (2024–2025: new child, business intensity, relationship challenges), Kagiso rebuilt a structured daily recovery protocol (10 min/day: morning intention + breathing, midday memory replay, evening reflection).
- Uses externalised systems as primary memory support — not relying on unaided recall.
- Key recovery principle: system design beats willpower. Builds scaffolding, not self-discipline.
