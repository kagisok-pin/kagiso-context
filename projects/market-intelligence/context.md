## What This Is

Consolidated market intelligence workstream covering product competitiveness analysis and competitor benchmarking for Pinnacle Stone and Nolte Sandton. Previously two separate tasks in Open Brain — now one initiative.

## Active: Product Mix Competitive Study

### Origin
Triggered by the Mirage online meeting (19 March 2026) with Alex Scorzoni and Francesco Forese (+ Wanela). The meeting surfaced questions about whether Pinnacle Stone's overall product offering is competitive and consistent with local SA market expectations.

### Scope
This is NOT just a Mirage assessment — it covers ALL product lines:
- **Pinnacle Quartz** — quartz surfaces (core brand)
- **ALTA Ceramica** — sub-brand for ceramics/porcelain
- **Mirage** — Italian porcelain tiles
- **Gardenia Orchidea** — Italian ceramics

For each line, assess against local competitors on:
- Product range breadth and depth
- Pricing positioning (premium vs mid-market vs budget)
- Market fit (does the SA market want what we're selling?)
- Distribution reach
- Brand awareness and perception
- Gaps and opportunities

### Competitor Analysis (Absorbed)
The broader competitor analysis task (captured 11 March) is absorbed into this study. That task called for:
- Key competitors for both Pinnacle Stone and Nolte Kitchens
- Positioning, pricing, product range, distribution, marketing
- SA-market specific and actionable

**For Pinnacle Stone competitors:** Caesarstone, Silestone (Cosentino), Cambria, local fabricators and importers, Tile Africa / CTM (retail channel), Italian Tile & Stone (importer)

**For Nolte Sandton competitors:** LEICHT Küchen via Inside Living (top Gauteng competitor per INDEX.md), Poggenpohl, SieMatic, local kitchen studios
