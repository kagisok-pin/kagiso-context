## What It Is

Parent-level platform concept and group architecture. Not consumer-facing (at this stage). The structural vision connecting operating companies into an integrated ecosystem.

**Name:** Arc (design line, architecture, aspiration) + Mantle (geology/stone, structure, foundation). Category-agnostic, bankable, discreet.

---

## Core Thesis

Value leaks at handoffs in the interiors value chain (Specification → Supply → Fabrication → Installation → Aftercare). Arc & Mantle reduces friction, captures margin across stages, increases accountability, and raises quality through integrated ownership. Long-term wealth architecture, not short-term expansion.

## Proposed Holding Structure

```
Family Trust
    └── Arc & Mantle Holdings (Pty) Ltd [HoldCo]
        ├── Pinnacle Stone [OpCo 1 — supply/distribution]
        ├── Nolte Kitchens GP [OpCo 2 — design/retail]
        ├── Future OpCos (OutdoorWorks, etc.)
        ├── IPCo [trademarks, brand, photography; licenses to OpCos]
        └── Shared Services [finance, HR, IT, RevOps, legal, procurement]
```

Investment committee rubric: 6 criteria scored 1–5; greenlight if avg ≥ 4 and payback ≤ 18 months. Published intercompany transfer prices (quarterly reviews); kill rules tied to KPIs.

## Operating Principles

1. **Vertical integration** — control multiple value chain stages
2. **Brand firewalling** — independent identities in market; no "house of brands" perception
3. **Operational synergy** — shared back-office (finance, HR, CRM, reporting); invisible to clients
4. **Quality as moat** — end-to-end ownership enables higher standards
5. **Long-term compounding** — reinvest cash flow, prioritise equity value over extraction

**Firewall discipline:** No bundling, no group discounts, no overt cross-selling, no cross-selling quotas. OpCos operate independently in market.

---

## Key Risks

1. Complexity overwhelms execution (too many initiatives)
2. Brand dilution (firewall breaks)
3. Capital strain (expansion outpaces cash flow)
4. Talent mismatch across OpCos
5. Founder capacity — Kagiso running CEO + CSO + systems architect + brand across multiple entities
