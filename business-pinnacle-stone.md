## What It Is

**Products:** Quartz, granite, porcelain slabs, sintered stone, large format tiles (via ALTA Ceramica). Italian exclusives: Mirage, Gardenia Orchidea (both exclusive SA distribution).

## Business Model

**Primary:** Importer/distributor. Trade-focused with retail exposure.

**Customer segments:** Fabricators (B2B, key relationship — Pinnacle supplies, does not compete), interior designers, contractors, direct retail (smaller component).

**Revenue drivers:** Volume sales to trade, margin on premium/specialty products, service quality driving repeat business.

**Margin context:** Pure slab sales 15–20%; full-service (slab + fabrication + install coordination) 35%+ but requires showroom/marketing/fitting overhead.
